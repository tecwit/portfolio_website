About Me

I am Antonio Rocha

I am a proud first generation college senior studying computer science at Northwestern University. I wish to use my developing skills in computing to solve modern-day problems, such as, the lack of computer software accessibility in minority populations. Modern technology has a wide reach but it has constraints which often limit the number of individuals it can serve. As a software engineer, I believe my experiences can contribute in closing the remaining gaps.


I am also passionate about increasing the representation of Latine people within the tech industry, especially within leadership positions. My goal is to achieve these roles to enable my community to do the same and encourage a positive and diverse work culture.
